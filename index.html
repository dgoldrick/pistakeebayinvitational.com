Our Story

A Tradition Worth Keeping

What began in the 1950s as a gathering of men on the shores of Pistakee Bay has grown into one of our family's most cherished institutions. Grandfathers taught fathers. Fathers taught sons. And every year, without fail, we return to the course — to compete, to tell the same stories, and to remind ourselves who we come from.
